Getting Started with Hydroponics

1. Choose Your System

Select from various hydroponic systems:

  • Deep Water Culture (DWC)
  • Nutrient Film Technique (NFT)
  • Ebb and Flow
  • Aeroponics

2. Essential Components

Gather the necessary equipment:

  • Growing container
  • Nutrient solution
  • pH meter
  • TDS meter
  • Growing medium

3. Monitor Parameters

Keep track of crucial growing conditions:

  • pH levels (5.5-6.5)
  • TDS (500-3500 ppm)
  • Turbidity
  • Temperature

Understanding pH and Nutrients

Maintaining proper pH levels is crucial for plant health:

  • Most plants prefer pH between 5.5 and 6.5
  • Use pH Up/Down solutions to adjust levels
  • Monitor pH daily for best results

TDS and Nutrient Management

Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) indicates nutrient concentration:

  • Different plants require different TDS levels
  • Regular monitoring prevents nutrient burn
  • Adjust nutrient solution strength as needed
